Projects

What Should We?

An Android app designed to help you anonymously make decisions. Can’t decide where to go out to eat, what movie to watch, or what board game to play? This little app helps you and your friends make a decision, so you don’t have to! Coming soon to Android and iOS.

Programming Challenges

During the summer of 2015 I completed all of the challenges posted to the dailyprogrammer subreddit. They post three challenges a week (easy, intermediate, and hard) and sometimes each of the three challenges build on top of each other to slowly build up one larger project. It’s a great challenge for learning a new programming language or honing your skills in a familiar language and I would highly recommend checking it out.
